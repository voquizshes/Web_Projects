What is an endangered animal?

Endangered animals are species that face a very high risk of extinction in the wild. Their numbers are shrinking because of threats like habitat loss, climate change, pollution, and hunting. Every endangered animal plays a vital role in keeping ecosystems healthy and balanced.

Why it matters

When we lose a species, we lose part of our world’s natural beauty and stability. Protecting endangered animals helps protect our future too.
